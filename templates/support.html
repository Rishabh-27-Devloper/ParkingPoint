Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a parking slot?
Go to Book Parking, select your location, date, and vehicle, then confirm your booking.
How can I cancel or modify my booking?
Visit My Bookings, choose the booking you want to change, and follow the instructions to cancel or modify.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking for online payments.
I have an issue with my payment. What should I do?
Contact our support team via email or WhatsApp with your booking details and payment reference.
How do I add or remove a vehicle?
Go to My Vehicles to manage your registered vehicles.
